# QI-FedDetect IEEE Access Upload Checklist

Target journal: IEEE Access

Recommended manuscript type: Research Article

Upload files:

- `01_Manuscript_Source_QI_FedDetect_IEEE_Access.docx`  
  Word source manuscript in double-column IEEE Access-style layout.

- `02_Manuscript_PDF_QI_FedDetect_IEEE_Access.pdf`  
  PDF rendered from the source manuscript. Content should match the Word source.

- `03_Cover_Letter_IEEE_Access.docx`  
  Cover letter.

- `04_Conflict_of_Interest_Statement.docx`  
  Conflict/declaration statement, if the portal requests a separate declaration.

- `05_Supplementary_Algorithm_Notes.md`  
  Optional supplementary notes describing the implementation-level algorithm updates.

Submission notes:

- Select manuscript type: Research Article.
- Use at least three keywords from the manuscript index terms.
- Confirm ORCID is public and linked to the submitting IEEE account.
- Confirm no simultaneous submission elsewhere.
- The manuscript includes an author biography below the references.
- The manuscript includes an AI disclosure statement and data availability statement.
- The Word source and PDF were rendered together for visual QA.
